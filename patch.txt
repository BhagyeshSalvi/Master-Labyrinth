How to Play :

1.Run the program enter as host and then enter port number 
2.Run again and then enter join game enter the IP (127.0.0.1 if same computer) and the port number.
3. Use the keyboards move up,down,left,right.
4.Player can only move if it his turn and if the tiles have paths to move.
5.Collect Tokens and your points increase.
6.After collecting points, end your turn and then next player can play
